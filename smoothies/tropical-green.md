# Tropical Green

Serves 2. Spinach hides under mango and pineapple — you get the greens without tasting them.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups Fresh spinach, packed
- 1 cup Frozen mango chunks
- 1 cup Frozen pineapple chunks
- 1 cup Coconut water
- 1 Banana, frozen
- 1 tablespoon Fresh lime juice
- 1/2-inch Fresh ginger, peeled
- 1 tablespoon Chia seeds (optional)

## Instructions

- Add the spinach, coconut water, and lime juice to the blender first
- Blend until the spinach is completely broken down, about 30 seconds
- Add the frozen mango, pineapple, banana, and ginger
- Blend on high for another 45 seconds until smooth
- Stir in chia seeds if using
- Pour into two glasses and serve immediately

## Tips

- Blending the greens with liquid first prevents leafy bits from ending up in the finished smoothie
- The mango-to-pineapple ratio is what tames the ginger; don't skimp on the frozen fruit
- A high-powered blender (Vitamix, Blendtec) gets the smoothest result; standard blenders work but leave more texture

## Variations

- Kale instead of spinach: use 1 cup packed, stems removed
- Higher protein: add 1 scoop vanilla protein powder or 2 tablespoons hemp hearts
- Extra creamy: swap 1/2 cup coconut water for 1/2 cup coconut milk
