# Papaya Paradise

Serves 2. Papaya blended with banana and coconut milk — smooth, tropical, and gently sweet.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups Ripe papaya, cubed (about 1/2 medium papaya)
- 1 cup Coconut milk (canned)
- 1 Banana, frozen
- 1 tablespoon Fresh lime juice
- 1 tablespoon Honey
- 1/4 teaspoon Vanilla extract
- 1/2 cup Ice
- Fresh mint leaves (optional, for garnish)

## Instructions

- Add the papaya, coconut milk, frozen banana, lime juice, honey, and vanilla to a blender
- Blend on high for 45 seconds until smooth
- Add ice and blend another 15 seconds
- Taste and adjust honey — ripe papaya is naturally sweet but tartness varies
- Pour into two glasses and garnish with mint if using

## Tips

- Ripe papaya smells fragrant and yields slightly to gentle pressure; underripe papaya is chalky and flavorless
- Lime juice cuts the mild funk that some people notice with papaya — do not skip it
- Freezing the papaya (rather than the banana) is an option if you prefer a thicker, colder texture

## Variations

- Yogurt version: swap 1/2 cup coconut milk for 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
- Vitamin C boost: add 1/4 cup fresh orange juice with the coconut milk
- Papaya-mango: add 1/2 cup frozen mango for extra tropical depth
