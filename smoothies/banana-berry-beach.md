# Banana Berry Beach

Serves 2. A milder tropical — banana, strawberry, pineapple, and coconut milk. Works for kids and adults.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup Frozen strawberries
- 1 cup Frozen pineapple chunks
- 2 Bananas, frozen
- 1 cup Coconut milk (canned or carton)
- 1/2 cup Vanilla Greek yogurt
- 1 tablespoon Honey (optional)
- 1/4 cup Ice

## Instructions

- Add all frozen fruit to the blender
- Add coconut milk, yogurt, and honey
- Blend on high for 45 seconds until smooth
- Add ice and blend another 15 seconds for extra thickness
- Pour into two glasses; top with a few sliced strawberries for garnish

## Tips

- Ripe (spotty) bananas add sweetness that lets you skip or reduce the honey
- If using carton coconut milk, expect a thinner smoothie; canned coconut milk is much richer

## Variations

- Mixed berry: swap 1/2 cup strawberries for 1/2 cup mixed berries
- Vegan: use coconut yogurt or omit yogurt and increase coconut milk to 1 1/4 cups
- Peanut butter tropical: add 2 tablespoons peanut butter with the yogurt
