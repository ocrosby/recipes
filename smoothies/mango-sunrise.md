# Mango Sunrise

Serves 2. Bright, sunny, and sweet — mango and orange juice with a banana base.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups Frozen mango chunks
- 1 cup Fresh orange juice (about 3 oranges)
- 1 Banana, frozen
- 1/2 cup Greek yogurt (plain or vanilla)
- 1 tablespoon Honey (optional)
- 1/4 teaspoon Fresh ginger, grated (optional, for warmth)

## Instructions

- Add the frozen mango, orange juice, banana, and yogurt to a blender
- Add the honey and grated ginger
- Blend on high for 45 seconds until completely smooth
- If too thick, add more orange juice; if too thin, add more frozen mango
- Pour into two glasses and serve immediately

## Tips

- Fresh-squeezed orange juice makes a real difference; carton juice works but tastes flatter
- The ginger is subtle but adds a background warmth that keeps this from being one-note sweet

## Variations

- Dairy-free: swap Greek yogurt for coconut yogurt
- Extra citrus: add zest from one orange before blending
- Peach mango: swap 1 cup mango for 1 cup frozen peaches
