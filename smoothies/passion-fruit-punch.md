# Passion Fruit Punch

Serves 2. Tart, tropical, and vibrantly colored — passion fruit is the star, with mango and pineapple to balance.

## Ingredients

- 3-4 Fresh passion fruits (or 1/3 cup passion fruit pulp / frozen puree)
- 1 cup Frozen mango chunks
- 1 cup Frozen pineapple chunks
- 1 cup Coconut water
- 1 tablespoon Fresh lime juice
- 1 tablespoon Honey or agave (adjust to taste)
- 1 Banana (optional, for a creamier texture)

## Instructions

- Halve the passion fruits and scoop the pulp (seeds included) into the blender
- Add the frozen mango, pineapple, coconut water, lime juice, and honey
- Add the banana if using
- Blend on high for 45 seconds
- Strain through a fine-mesh sieve if you prefer no seeds; otherwise pour directly
- Serve immediately

## Tips

- Ripe passion fruit is heavy for its size and wrinkled on the outside — smooth ones aren't ready
- Frozen passion fruit puree (Latin markets or Amazon) is a reliable substitute when fresh isn't in season
- The passion fruit tartness is bright — you may want more honey than the recipe calls for

## Variations

- Cocktail version: add 2 oz white rum and 1 oz Campari; serve over ice
- Extra tropical: add 1/2 cup fresh papaya
- Yogurt version: replace 1/2 cup coconut water with 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
