# Piña Colada Smoothie

Serves 2. The classic beach-in-a-glass — pineapple, coconut, and just enough banana to keep it thick.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups Fresh pineapple, cubed and frozen
- 1 cup Coconut milk (canned, full-fat for creaminess)
- 1 Banana, frozen and sliced
- 2 tablespoons Coconut cream
- 1 tablespoon Honey or maple syrup (optional)
- 1 tablespoon Fresh lime juice
- 1/2 cup Ice

## Instructions

- Add the frozen pineapple, banana, coconut milk, and coconut cream to a blender
- Add the honey and lime juice
- Blend on high for 30-45 seconds until completely smooth
- Add ice and blend another 15 seconds
- Taste and adjust honey to preference
- Pour into two glasses and serve immediately

## Tips

- Freezing the pineapple and banana ahead of time gives the smoothie a thick, frosty texture without watering it down with too much ice
- If you don't have coconut cream, use extra coconut milk plus a splash of heavy cream

## Variations

- Cocktail version: add 2 oz white rum after blending
- Green: add 1 cup fresh spinach with the fruit (color muted by pineapple; nutrition intact)
- Extra tropical: swap 1/2 cup pineapple for 1/2 cup mango
