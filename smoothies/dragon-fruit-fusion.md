# Dragon Fruit Fusion

Serves 2. Bright magenta from red dragon fruit (pitaya), with mango and coconut water for a light tropical finish.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup Red dragon fruit, cubed (fresh or frozen; about 1 medium pitaya)
- 1 cup Frozen mango chunks
- 1 cup Coconut water
- 1 Banana, frozen
- 1/2 cup Vanilla Greek yogurt
- 1 tablespoon Fresh lime juice
- 1 tablespoon Honey

## Instructions

- Add the dragon fruit, mango, coconut water, and banana to the blender
- Add the yogurt, lime juice, and honey
- Blend on high for 45 seconds until smooth
- If too thick, thin with more coconut water; if too thin, add more frozen fruit
- Pour into two glasses — expect a striking magenta color

## Tips

- Red dragon fruit (pitaya) gives the vibrant color; white dragon fruit works flavor-wise but yields a pale pink or beige smoothie
- Fresh dragon fruit should smell faintly sweet and yield slightly under gentle pressure; frozen pitaya packets are a reliable substitute
- The color transfers to anything it touches (tongue, cup, counter) — pour with care and rinse the blender quickly

## Variations

- Tropical berry: add 1/2 cup mixed berries with the dragon fruit
- Green pitaya: use white dragon fruit + 1 cup fresh spinach for a green smoothie base
- Extra tang: swap 1/2 cup coconut water for 1/2 cup fresh orange juice
