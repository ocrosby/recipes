# Kiwi Colada

Serves 2. A green twist on Piña Colada — tart kiwi replaces the pineapple lead, with coconut and lime for balance.

## Ingredients

- 4 ripe Kiwis, peeled and quartered
- 1 cup Frozen pineapple chunks
- 1/2 cup Coconut cream
- 1/2 cup Coconut milk
- 1 Banana, frozen
- 1 tablespoon Fresh lime juice
- 2 tablespoons Honey
- 1/2 cup Ice

## Instructions

- Add the kiwis, frozen pineapple, coconut cream, and coconut milk to the blender
- Add the banana, lime juice, and honey
- Blend on high for 45 seconds until smooth
- Add ice and blend another 15 seconds
- Pour into two glasses; the color will be a soft pale green

## Tips

- Kiwi seeds get slightly crushed in the blender — if you want a fully smooth drink, strain through a fine-mesh sieve before serving
- Ripe kiwi yields to gentle pressure and smells slightly floral; underripe kiwi will make the smoothie unpleasantly tart
- Actinidin (an enzyme in kiwi) can thin dairy over time — drink this one within 30 minutes if you used yogurt

## Variations

- Cocktail version: add 2 oz white rum
- With mint: add 4-5 fresh mint leaves for a mojito-adjacent finish
- Kiwi-mango: swap 1/2 cup pineapple for 1/2 cup frozen mango
