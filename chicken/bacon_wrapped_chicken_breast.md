# Bacon Wrapped Chicken Breast

Serves 4.

A sheet-pan dinner: seared bacon-wrapped chicken roasted over Yukon gold potato medallions, with broccoli added in the last 15 minutes.

## Ingredients

### Chicken

- 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
- 12-16 slices bacon (3-4 per breast)
- Pompeian Robust Extra Virgin Olive Oil
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Paprika
- Garlic powder
- Onion powder

### Potatoes

- Yukon gold potatoes, cut into medallions
- Extra virgin olive oil
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Dried Mediterranean oregano
- Dried sage
- Dried rosemary

### Broccoli

- Broccoli florets
- Extra virgin olive oil
- Salt
- Black pepper
- 1-2 tablespoons butter (plus more for finishing)

## Instructions

### Preheat

- Preheat the oven to 375°F

### Prep the sheet pan

- Lay parchment paper on a baking sheet
- Splash a little water under the parchment so it sticks flat to the pan

### Season and sear the chicken

- Pat the chicken breasts very dry
- Place them in a bowl and coat with Pompeian Robust extra virgin olive oil
- Season with salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder, and onion powder
- Heat a stainless steel pan until hot
- Sear the chicken on both sides until well browned

### Wrap with bacon

- Stretch each slice of bacon by running the back of a knife along it — this thins it out and reduces how many slices you need
- Wrap each seared breast with 3-4 stretched bacon slices

### Prep the potatoes

- Cut Yukon gold potatoes into medallion shapes
- In a mixing bowl, toss the medallions with extra virgin olive oil, salt, pepper, dried Mediterranean oregano, dried sage, and dried rosemary

### Assemble and roast

- Lay the seasoned potato medallions out on the parchment as a base
- Place the bacon-wrapped chicken on top of the potatoes
- Roast at 375°F for 35 minutes

### Add the broccoli

- While the chicken is in the oven, place broccoli in a bowl
- Season with extra virgin olive oil, salt, pepper, and 1-2 tablespoons of butter, then mix
- After 35 minutes, pull the sheet out and nestle the broccoli in between the chicken
- Add additional butter on top
- Return to the oven for another 15 minutes

## Tips

- Pat the chicken bone-dry before oiling — a wet surface won't sear, it will steam
- Stretching the bacon is the key to wrapping with fewer slices and getting it to lay flat
- The potatoes act as a roasting rack — they crisp underneath while catching the bacon drippings

## Storage

- Refrigerator: up to 3 days in an airtight container
- Reheating: oven at 350°F for 10-15 minutes to keep the bacon crisp; avoid the microwave
