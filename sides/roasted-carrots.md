# Roasted Carrots

Sweet, tender roasted carrots with caramelized edges. A great contrast to savory pork.

---

## Ingredients

* 1½ lb carrots, cut evenly
* Olive oil
* Kosher salt
* Optional: honey, thyme, black pepper

---

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to **425°F (220°C)**.
2. Toss carrots with olive oil and salt.
3. Spread evenly on a baking sheet.
4. Roast **20–25 minutes**, turning once, until tender and lightly caramelized.
5. Optional finish: drizzle with honey and sprinkle with herbs.

---

## Notes

* Cut carrots evenly for consistent cooking.
* A light honey glaze pairs well with pork.
