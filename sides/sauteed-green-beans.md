# Sautéed Green Beans

Fast, fresh, and lightly blistered green beans that balance rich main dishes like pork, steak, or chicken.

---

## Ingredients

* 1 lb fresh green beans, trimmed
* 1–2 tbsp olive oil or butter
* Kosher salt, to taste
* Freshly cracked black pepper (optional)
* Optional additions:

  * 1–2 cloves garlic, minced
  * Lemon juice or zest
  * Red pepper flakes

---

## Instructions

1. Heat a skillet over **medium-high heat** and add olive oil or butter.
2. Add green beans in a **single layer**.
3. Cook **5–7 minutes**, tossing occasionally, until blistered and tender-crisp.
4. Add garlic during the **last 30 seconds** if using.
5. Season with salt (and pepper, if desired).
6. Finish with a squeeze of lemon or pinch of red pepper flakes.

---

## Notes

* Do not overcrowd the pan or the beans will steam.
* Beans should remain bright green with light char spots.
* Works well finished with a touch of pork pan sauce.

---
