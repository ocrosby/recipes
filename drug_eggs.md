# Drug Eggs

## Ingredients

- Eggs
- Garlic
- Ginger
- Green Onion
- Sesame Seeds
- Sesame Oil
- Red Chilli
- Soy Sauce

## Instructions

- Boil Eggs for 7 minutes
- Chop the ingredeients (minus the eggs)
- Place the ingredients in a container
- Add Soy Sauce
- Add Sesame Oil
- Add Seasame Seeds
