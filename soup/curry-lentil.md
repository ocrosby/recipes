# Curry Lentil Soup

## Ingredients

- 1 Onion, chopped
- 1 Carrot, cut into discs
- 2-3 Garlic Cloves, grated
- 1 thumb of Ginger, grated
- 2 tablespoons Olive oil
- 1 tablespoon Curry powder
- 1 tablespoon Tumeric powder
- 1 tablespoon Ground cumin
- 1 tablespoon Red pepper flakes
- 1 cup Brown or green lentils, rinsed
- Vegetable broth
- 1 can Crushed tomatoes
- 2 Potatoes, peeled and diced
- Kale, chopped
- Salt to taste
- Pepper to taste

## Instructions

- Gently fry the chopped onion and carrot discs in olive oil
- Add the grated garlic and ginger
- Add the curry powder, tumeric, ground cumin, and red pepper flakes
- Fry for 2 more minutes
- Rinse the lentils and add them to the pot
- Add the vegetable broth
- Add the can of crushed tomatoes
- Add the peeled and diced potatoes
- Add the chopped kale
- Season with salt and pepper
- Let the soup simmer for 30 minutes until the lentils are cooked
